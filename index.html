About GORUCO

The Gotham Ruby Conference (GORUCO) is a one-day event that will take place in New York City on June 21, 2014. It is a technical conference aimed at highly motivated programmers interested in all things Ruby.

You'll spend the day among Rubyists, Rails developers, and language enthusiasts in New York City, home of Silicon Alley.
