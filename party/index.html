Yacht Party

We're incredibly excited about this year's GORUCO afterparty, and expect it to be the best yet. Much fun was had by all aboard the GORUCO boat party last year, so this year we're continuing that tradition but stepping it up.

This year's afterparty is a two hour cruise around New York City aboard the new Hornblower Infinity 210 foot yacht. The boat cruise will feature an open bar as well as light food. All GORUCO attendees receive admission to the boat party for themselves and a guest.

Directions

The boat will depart from Hornblower Landing at Pier 40 in Manhattan. The address is 353 West Street (also known as West Side Highway) at West Houston Street, New York, NY.
