Sponsorship Opportunities

This year GORUCO will be bigger and better than ever. GORUCO 2012 is going to be hosted at the Altman Building in the Flatiron district of Manhattan. Our new location has over 13,000 square feet of space and we are expecting 275 Ruby developers to attend.

A Conference for Motivated Programmers

The Gotham Ruby Conference (GORUCO) is a one-day event that will take place in New York City on June 23, 2012. It is a technical conference aimed at highly motivated programmers interested in all things Ruby. Most of our attendees live in the Tri-State area.
